Vegarden is a veggie gardening database and management app designed to improve and enhace your health by allowing you to grow better and healthier veggies.
Dig into the veggie database to know more about them. Each vegetable has a lot of useful information and a personalized approach to gardening, with information about the potential problems and diseases, planting directions, planting companions, growing and harvest tips, storage and use.


Features: 

- Browse through our well cared database
- Add vegetables to My Crops to have an easy and quick access
- 
- Learn the tips/tricks of growing vegetables 
- Track progress of your planted veggies
